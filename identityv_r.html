About Me

Although I can play both survivor and hunter, I prefer survivor more. I like to play these mode: Ranked, Quick Match, and Duo Hunter. I don't play much of the other modes.


Please feel free to invite me to team up or add me as a friend in the game! I will be glad to play with you and have fun together. However, I am not a professional player and may not be the best at the game. I may make mistakes sometimes, but I will try my best to play well and have fun with you!

Favourite Character

Florian Brand

The Fire Investigator

This is my favorite character in Identity V.

I like Florian not only because he is cute and cool, but also because of his tragic backstory, which makes me sympathize with him.


If you read Character Day letter carefully, you will found that he is actually a kind-hearted and caring character by nature. His darker and more unstable side only appeared because things happened at the orphanage. Although tragic backstories are a common storytelling technique, Florian’s profession, his former warm home, and his personality, behavior, and aspirations make him truly special to me. I also like his two-faced complexity and moral ambiguity, with the contrast on his kindness and hidden darkness.


To Conclude: At first, I only thought Florian looked cool because of the fire investigator design. But after reading his story and personality, Florian slowly became my one true favorite character in Identity V.


Anyway, who can refuse to like a kind, handsome firefighter with a charming smile?
